About Brandon

Early Life

Greetings Everyone!
My name is Brandon Lee Red Shirt. I am 33 years old. And I already know you may have a couple of questions. So let me take this time to asnwer them for you. Yes my last name is Native American. I am part of the Oglala Sioux tribe from the Pine Ridge Reservation in south west South Dakota. Also, I am related to an actual Chief. I was born in Denver. I grew up in the La Alma neighborhood of off Colfax and Kalamath. I graduated from Denver South High School in 2003.

Adult Life

After high school I took a two year hiatus working odd jobs until I decided to get my life together and joined the military. In the military I was a AH-64D Apache Attack Helicopter mechanic. During my six year contract I was deployed to Iraq and Afghanistan. Also, I was stationed overseas in the Republic of Korea for a year. When my initial contract was completed I joined the Army Reserve and became a Warrant Officer to fly UH-60M Blackhawk helicopters. After I separated from the military I went out and earned my civilian Airframe and Powerplant certification to perform maintenance legally on aircraft. I moved to Germany for work as a helicopter mechanic. I was based out of small medieval town called Rothenberg ob der Tauber. After my contract was up I moved back to the U.S. to complete some educational goals and this is where I find myself at Galvanize now.
